About the Youth Centre

The Youth Centre was established in January 2021 with the mission of empowering young individuals through education, skill development, and community engagement. It serves as a hub for creativity, innovation, and collaboration among the youth.

Our Activities

  • Workshops on leadership and personal development
  • Skill-building programs in technology, arts, and sports
  • Community service and volunteer opportunities
  • Networking events and mentorship programs
